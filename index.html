Alexander Kuhn

Mathematics, to me, is a language of structure and precision: abstract, yet remarkably effective at describing the world around us. It helps uncover patterns, explain complexity, and model processes across nature and technology. What drives me is the point where theory meets application. I enjoy working with equations and models that capture real phenomena, especially in dynamical systems, and turning them into insights or tools that make a difference beyond the blackboard. I care about clarity more than credentials. I want to meet people where they are, explain things in a way that makes sense, and earn understanding rather than expect it.

Academic Specialisation

  • Mathematical Modelling and Simulation of Natural Processes
  • Theory and Numerics of Differential Equations (ODEs and PDEs)
